2 BEDROOMS | 4 BEDS | 2 BATH | SLEEP 3-6 GUESTS

Discover the charm of our 1792 cabin nestled within the breathtaking Smoky Mountains, just minutes away from the vibrant town of Pigeon Forge. With two spacious bedrooms, four comfortable beds, and two pristine bathrooms, our cabin offers the perfect retreat for groups of 3-6 guests. Surrounded by the stunning beauty of the Smokies, you can wake up to the serene sounds of nature and savor your morning coffee on the private porch.

But that's not all! Our cabin isn't just a place to relax; it's a gateway to adventure in the heart of the Smoky Mountains. Explore the nearby hiking trails, take a refreshing dip in the crystal-clear creek, or simply unwind by the cozy fireplace in the evenings. For history enthusiasts, our cabin's rustic charm and meticulously preserved 18th-century details will transport you back in time. Come and experience the magic of Red Bone Cabins' 1792 retreat in the magnificent Smoky Mountains near Pigeon Forge, and create memories that will last a lifetime.
